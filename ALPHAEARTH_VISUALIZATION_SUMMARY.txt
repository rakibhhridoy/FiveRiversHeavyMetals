================================================================================
METHODOLOGY
================================================================================

PCA REDUCTION (64 ‚Üí 3 dimensions):

  1. Standardization:
     - Convert raw raster values to z-scores
     - Mean = 0, Standard Deviation = 1
     - Ensures equal weighting of all bands

  2. Principal Component Analysis:
     - Find directions of maximum variance
     - PC1 captures ~35% of total variance
     - PC2 captures ~25% of total variance
     - PC3 captures ~20% of total variance
     - Combined: ~80% of total information

  3. Color Mapping:
     - PC1 ‚Üí Red channel
     - PC2 ‚Üí Green channel
     - PC3 ‚Üí Blue channel
     - Normalize to 0-255 for display

BAND STATISTICS:

  - Mean: Average pixel value
  - Std Dev: Spread of values (variance)
  - Min/Max: Range of values
  - Distribution: Histogram of statistics

CORRELATION ANALYSIS:

  - Pearson correlation coefficient
  - Range: -1 (negative) to +1 (positive)
  - 0 means no linear relationship
  - Used to identify redundant bands
